About



Develop-Verse is a 28 hrs Hackathon which mainly focuses on development side to solve a real life problem statement. The main motive of this event is to provide them a platform where developers can apply their creative thought process and Skills to create a solution for an existing problem. The participants will get to explore the various areas of development and it will ultimately solve the real-life problems which will involve out of the box thinking.
